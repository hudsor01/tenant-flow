<objective>
Strengthen the blog generator (`scripts/generate-blog-draft.ts`) so drafts are on-brand,
E-E-A-T-credible, and grounded in retrieved RAG facts only, then add an LLM-as-judge
self-critique gate that rejects thin or off-brand drafts BEFORE they reach `in-review`.

Purpose: Closes BLOG-06 (brand voice + E-E-A-T + RAG-grounding enforced in the prompt) and
the self-critique half of BLOG-07 (BLOG-07a). The deterministic 9 gates already catch
structural failures (word count, H2s, banlist); they cannot judge whether the prose is
genuinely helpful, on-brand, or factually grounded. The judge is the quality gate the gates
can't be.

Output: A modified generator with (1) a hardened system prompt and (2) a bounded
critique→regenerate loop after the gates pass and before the HMAC POST, plus unit tests
proving the judge rejects a thin/off-brand sample and admits a good one.
</objective>
