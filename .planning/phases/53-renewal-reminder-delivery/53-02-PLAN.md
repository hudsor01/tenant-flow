| T-53-04 | Tampering | open redirect via action_url | mitigate | app-relative `/leases/<id>` only; resolveHref already rejects `//`/backslash/control chars |

| T-53-02 | Information disclosure | raw err.message to client/logs | mitigate | `errorResponse`/`captureWebhookError` — generic client body, Sentry-only detail; sendEmail never throws |
| T-53-SC | Supply chain | package installs | accept | No npm/Deno package additions (zero-new-deps invariant) — nothing to audit |
