<objective>
DATA-03: the Expired lease tile is wrong at two layers. (1) `get_lease_stats` computes `'expiredLeases'` from `lease_status = 'ended'`, but the `expire-leases` cron (`20260222120000`) sets naturally-lapsed leases to `lease_status = 'expired'` — so cron-expired leases are never counted. (2) The frontend `LeaseStatus` union in `core.ts:13` is missing `'expired'`, even though the DB CHECK constraint (`leases_lease_status_check`) allows it. Widening the union ripples to every exhaustive `Record<LeaseStatus>` consumer (same discipline as MAINT-06). This plan fixes the RPC filter and widens the union with full lockstep coverage.

Purpose: The Expired tile counts cron-expired leases, and every lease-status display surface renders `expired` correctly with a green typecheck.
Output: One migration recreating `get_lease_stats` (applied via MCP, filename reconciled, rolled-back live proof) plus the union widening and all lockstep-site edits. No `src/types/supabase.ts` regen (RPC returns jsonb — signature unchanged; `LeaseStatus` is a hand-maintained union, not generated).
</objective>
